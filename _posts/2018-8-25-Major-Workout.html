Every workout we produce is about body control. They help you get stronger, faster, more flexible so you can then use your body better than you ever have before. Major is a workout that takes this to a new level through its combination of exercises that target overlapping muscle groups in strict sequence. The result is a difficulty Level III workout that actually feels way harder.
