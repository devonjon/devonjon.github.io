The Leap uses gravity and dynamic movements to load the muscles with two to three times the body's weight. The result is a workout that feels light and fun but will also get you into the sweatzone fast, making strong demands on your VO2 Max capacity. It also helps develop the kind of lower body and core strength you need to level up. Core and legs power everything so be prepared to put in perfect form.
