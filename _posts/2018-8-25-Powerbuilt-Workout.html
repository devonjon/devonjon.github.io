Powerbuilt may not look difficult but as a difficulty Level IV workout it is one that will let you know immediately that you've started to build up a load. Many muscle groups are recruited for each exercise making this a go-to choice for building up muscle strength. Pay attention to form and stay focused throughout. Your body will know the difference the day after.
