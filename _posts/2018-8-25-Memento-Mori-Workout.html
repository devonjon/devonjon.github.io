Memento Mori is not a massively difficult workout but that doesn't make it an easy one either. It all depends upon the exercises, pace and rhythm your body is used to. If it challenges you on many levels it may end up feeling harder than it at first looks, especially if you add EC to it all. Maintain perfect form and make sure you're working your muscles fully.
