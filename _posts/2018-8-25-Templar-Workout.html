Take four exercises that demand dynamic core/abs work. Add lower/upper body loads through combat moves and ballistic exercises. Templar is the outcome of that particular mix. It is only Level III on the DAREBEE scale of difficulty but it's designed to make you work as hard as you push. So push as hard as you can.
