When High Knees is the core element around which a workout revolves you know you have an aerobically intensive session on your hands and Gipsy does not disappoint. Give your lungs the workout they crave by working fast on each exercise without breaking form. Up the intensity to the maximum you can handle and try to maintain it at that level throughout the session.
