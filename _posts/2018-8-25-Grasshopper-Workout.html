Use your body to fight gravity and develop the kind of elastic, explosive, lean muscle that makes you feel like you can do anything. Grasshopper is just such a workout. It helps you develop fascial strength and it still takes you to the point where your VO2 Max is challenged. Add EC and you have a real winner on your hands.
