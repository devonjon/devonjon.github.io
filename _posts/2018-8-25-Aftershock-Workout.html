There are total body workouts that work all the muscles a little and there are others which work the whole body but pay particular attention to a specific group of muscles and then there's Aftershock. A difficulty Level IV workout that promises to make you vow to conquer it because it is a true challenge. Try it at the hardest setting you can (which really has to include EC) and come back to it time and again until you can truly master it.
