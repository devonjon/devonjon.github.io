Candy Crasher will work your legs, your lungs and even your arms and back but that's not really the reason you want to do it. What Candy Crasher does is teaches your body to move lightly on your feet and that is an awesome ability to have. Plus, it's the workout to do this Halloween. Add EC for that extra candy you ate.
