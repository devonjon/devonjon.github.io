King is a difficulty Level V upper body workout that targets all the upper body muscles in an exercise routine that helps build strength and power. Each exercise loads muscles that have to work again in the next one, working both concentrically and eccentrically for maximum effect. Add EC here and this workout becomes a serious challenge.
