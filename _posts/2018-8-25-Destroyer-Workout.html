The Destroyer is a full-body, strength workout that utilizes simple combat moves to deliver a solid workout that targets virtually every major muscle group in the body. Executed slowly, with near-perfect form, these moves recruit a number of primary and secondary muscle groups, engage the core, challenge the brain (that must model everything so all this can happen) and help develop better balance and coordination.
