Nothing says "Body Control" better than being able to drop and perform an one-arm push-up. Taking a large part of your body's weight on one arm is a sign of serious power. This is why you really need to put the One-Arm Push-Up Prep workout on your horizon and not give up until you've mastered it. Remember your shoulders have to be parallel to the floor and your core and abs must be kept tight throughout the exercises. And EC is something you really want to do here. Remember your goal!
