If you have access to a chin-up bar, a ledge and a floor you have the raw ingredients for the Power Builder workout. Designed to build real strength that you can convert into power, Power Builder is a workout to failure that will leave you feeling the burn. Add EC if you must but at difficulty Level IV this one's already tough to beat.
