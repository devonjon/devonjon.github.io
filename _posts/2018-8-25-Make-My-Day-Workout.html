If you want to move fast, change directions quickly, fight with devastating effectiveness, then you need to either relocate to a planet with a lower gravity or reduce the mass of your body, effectively making yourself lighter. The Make My Day workout does just that, by making you feel lighter. Successive exercise spit your bodyweight against gravity and you end up gasping for breath. This is not suitable for beginners.
